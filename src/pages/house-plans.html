Learn More About Our House Plans (Floor Plans & Construction Plans)

What are House Plans?

House plans are the complete technical and visual documents that turn your idea of a home into something a builder can price, approve and construct.

They include:

Floor Plans – The layout of rooms, walls, doors, windows and furniture footprints with accurate dimensions and levels.

Elevations – External views showing heights, finishes and materials.

Sections – Cut-through views explaining structure, floor-to-ceiling heights, stair details, insulation, etc.

Roof Plans – Slopes, drainage, truss indications and coverings.

Schedules – Doors, windows, finishes, fixtures.

Site Plans – Position on the plot, building lines, contours, services and north orientation.

Construction Plans/Details – Foundation/slab details, walls, lintels, stairs, waterproofing, energy notes and assembly specs.

Together, they form the blueprint for council approval, contractor pricing and on-site construction.
