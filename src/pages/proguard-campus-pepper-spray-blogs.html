Legal & safety reminder

Pepper spray is legal for self-defence. Use must be reasonable and proportional. Keep out of reach of children. Always check your university’s specific policy.

Institutions on Pepper Spray Products:
Some universities and residences restrict or prohibit visible pepper spray canisters. Crimson Veil (lipstick) and Fresh Guard (deodorant) were designed to look like everyday personal items so you can still take responsibility for your own safety without drawing attention.

Important disclaimer: Even disguised devices contain real pepper spray/OC gel and may still fall under your institution’s rules. Always check your university or residence policy first and consult campus security or student affairs if you are unsure. ProGuard encourages full compliance with all institutional regulations.
