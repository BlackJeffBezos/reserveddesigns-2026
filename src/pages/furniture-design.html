What is Furniture Design?

Furniture design is the creative and technical process of shaping everyday items like chairs, tables, cabinets and custom pieces into functional, beautiful and durable products. With CAD (Computer-Aided Design), we take furniture concepts from simple sketches or ideas and transform them into precise 3D models, realistic visualizations and production-ready drawings.

Whether it’s a modern minimalist piece, a statement design or a custom solution tailored to a unique space, CAD-driven furniture design ensures accuracy, efficiency and style all in one.
